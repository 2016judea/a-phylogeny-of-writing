Literature, read by machine.

Years spent asking what fiction looks like when you treat it as data — using NLP and network science to study prose, voice, and the shape of genre itself.

Columbia · Networks & Crowds · 2021

Literature Mutations

Genres are literature's main system of classification, yet the rate at which they form and mutate has only ever been judged by opinion. This models all of fiction as a growing network — each novel a node, each edge a genre similarity — to watch genres emerge from the data and measure how fast new ones appear.
